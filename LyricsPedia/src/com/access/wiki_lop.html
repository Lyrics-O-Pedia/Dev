Kaththi (English: Knife) is an upcoming Tamil action thriller film written and directed by AR Murugadoss.[2] The film stars Vijay in a dual role [3] alongside Samantha Ruth Prabhu[4] and Neil Nitin Mukesh, who debuts in Tamil cinema.[5][6] Anirudh Ravichander composes the music for the film.[7]
